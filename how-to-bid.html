Acquiring the Unique

A vertical journey from creation to possession.

I

The Edition Drop

A single garment is revealed. We release one edition at a time. Notification is immediate; the window of opportunity is finite.

The Private Bid

The gallery opens for bids. Values are placed blindly or openly, depending on the edition's protocol. The highest patron secures the right to own.

Certification

Secure, insured shipping worldwide. The edition arrives in a custom 1of1 flight case. Ownership is final.
